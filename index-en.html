ABOUT LEAN SUMMIT CHILE

Purpose

  • Understand how Lean thinking is applied in Chile in services, health, manufacturing, construction and mining.
  • Understand how to develop capabilities in business teams and help them to solve problems.
  • Understand the importance of leadership to start your Lean transformation and maintain it.
  • Give participants the opportunity to develop their own Lean network.

Why?

  • Provide a variety of applications for Lean Thinking in different industries, recognise good results, and also, how people have overcome difficulties in implementation.
  • Show the strengths of Lean Thinking at an international level and learn about experts from the Lean Global Network.
  • Demonstrate how developing the capabilities of people can achieve exceptional results for the client and the company.

Questions to Answer

  • What are the key elements to start Lean transformation?
  • Why does the development of peoples' capabilities generate value to the final client?
  • What do you do, to implement Lean in services, health and mining?
